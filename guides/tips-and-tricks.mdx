## Getting Started Tips

### First Launch Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initialize Audio">
    Wait for all samples to load (loading spinner), then click "Initialize Audio" to activate the audio engine. This is required by browsers for security.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Your Mode">
    Press `J` to open settings and choose an Auto DJ mode. Start with ATMOSPHERE for a balanced experience.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjust Base Volume">
    Set main track volume to 60-70% as a starting point before adding layers.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Layer Gradually">
    Add one ambient effect at a time (rain, thunder, etc.) to find your ideal mix. Avoid turning everything on at once.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Give each new setting 30-60 seconds before adjusting again. Some transitions take a few bars to fully develop.
</Tip>

## Volume Mixing Strategies

### The "Foundation First" Approach

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with Main Track">
    Set main track volume to 60-70%. This is your musical foundation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add One Environmental Layer">
    Choose rain (for focus) or campfire (for warmth) at 40-50% volume.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Complementary Effects">
    Thunder pairs well with rain (30-40%). Keep it subtle for dramatic accents.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Layer Ambient Tracks Sparingly">
    Add ONE ambient track (like waves or night ambience) at 30-40%. More than one can get muddy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Keyboard Efficiency

Maximize your workflow with these keyboard shortcuts:

### Essential Shortcuts

**The Power User Combo:**
1. `Space` - Quick play/pause (most important!)
2. `J` - Instant settings access
3. `K` - Emergency "stop all ambient tracks" button
4. `A` - Toggle rain (most-used effect)

<Note>

  - `9` - Underwater

## Auto DJ Optimization

### When to Use Each Mode

**MUSIC Mode:**
- Deep focus sessions (coding, writing)
- When you've manually set perfect environmental sounds
- Situations where sudden changes would be distracting
- Video calls (set it and forget it)

**ATMOSPHERE Mode:**
- General productivity work
- Study sessions
- Creative work where variety helps
- 2+ hour sessions where you want evolution without intervention

**WORLD Mode:**
- Exploration and discovery
- Background ambience for socializing
- When you want maximum variety
- Falling asleep (ever-changing but soothing)

**MANUAL Mode:**
- Recording or streaming (consistent sound)
- When you've found THE perfect combination
- Teaching or presenting (no surprises)
- Short sessions where you don't need transitions

<Tip>
  Start with MUSIC mode for your first 15 minutes. Once you know what environmental sounds you want, switch to ATMOSPHERE mode.
</Tip>

### Understanding Transitions

Auto DJ transitions happen every 16-48 bars (randomly chosen):

- **16 bars**: ~30 seconds (frequent changes, high variety)
- **32 bars**: ~60 seconds (balanced - default effective length)
- **48 bars**: ~90 seconds (slower evolution)

<Note>
  The transition includes a professional crossfade:
  1. Music gently muffles (2 seconds)
  2. New key/progression loads
  3. Music brightens back up (2 seconds)
  
  This 4-second transition is smooth and non-jarring.
</Note>

## Performance Optimization

### Browser Recommendations

**Best Performance:**
- Chrome/Edge (Chromium-based)
- Safari on macOS

**Good Performance:**
- Firefox
- Brave

<Tip>
  For best results, use a dedicated browser window or tab for LoFi Engine. Avoid having 50+ tabs open simultaneously.
</Tip>

### System Resource Tips

<Steps>
  <Step title="Close Unnecessary Apps">
    LoFi Engine uses real-time audio synthesis. Close other audio applications for optimal performance.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use Moderate Volume Levels">
    Very high volumes can cause clipping. Keep main track below 75% for clean sound.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Limit Custom Backgrounds">
    Keep custom background images under 5MB. Compress large images before uploading.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Limit Active Layers">
    3-5 simultaneous audio layers is optimal. More can cause CPU strain on older systems.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Desktop App vs Web Version

**Desktop App Advantages:**
- Dedicated process (better performance)
- System tray controls
- Native window management
- Reduced browser overhead

**Web Version Advantages:**
- No installation required
- Access from any device
- Automatic updates
- Lower disk space usage

<Note>
  Custom backgrounds and settings are stored per-browser. They don't sync between the desktop app and web version.
</Note>

## Creative Usage Patterns

### The "Pomodoro Mix" Technique

<Steps>
  <Step title="Focus Block (25 min)">
    MUSIC mode + Rain + Night Ambience. Consistent, non-distracting sound.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Break (5 min)">
    Switch to WORLD mode or manually change background and add new ambient track. Fresh environment for mental reset.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Next Block">
    Return to MUSIC mode but keep the new ambient combination. Familiar structure, slightly different flavor.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Long Break (15 min)">
    ATMOSPHERE mode + Campfire. Rewarding, cozy sound for rest.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### The "Context Switching" Method

Associate specific settings with different work types:

- **Writing**: Main track only, MUSIC mode
- **Coding**: Rain + Thunder, ATMOSPHERE mode, dark background
- **Meetings**: Mute main track, keep subtle rain at 30%
- **Research**: Campfire + Night Ambience, MUSIC mode

Your brain will start associating these soundscapes with their tasks, improving focus.

<Tip>
  Take quick "sound breaks" by pressing `K` to stop all ambient tracks for 10 seconds. The contrast makes returning to your soundscape feel fresh.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting & Quick Fixes

### Common Issues

<Accordion title="No Sound After Clicking Play">
  **Solutions:**
  - Ensure you clicked "Initialize Audio" first
  - Check your system volume and browser permissions
  - Refresh the page (Ctrl+R) and try again
  - Check that main track volume isn't at 1% (minimum)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Choppy or Laggy Audio">
  **Solutions:**
  - Close other audio applications
  - Reduce number of active layers (aim for 3-4)
  - Close other browser tabs
  - Restart the application
  - Try the desktop app for better performance
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Settings Not Saving">
  **Solutions:**
  - Check if browser is in incognito/private mode (localStorage disabled)
  - Ensure you have enough disk space
  - Clear cache and reload, then reconfigure
  - Check browser permissions for localStorage
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Custom Background Disappeared">
  **Solutions:**
  - Check if browser cleared IndexedDB (storage quota exceeded)
  - Re-upload the image
  - Use smaller images (under 5MB recommended)
</Accordion>

<Warning>
  If you're experiencing persistent issues, try clearing your browser cache and localStorage. Note that this will reset all your settings to defaults.
</Warning>

## Advanced Techniques

### The "Refresh Strategy"

The refresh button (below play button) generates a new chord progression instantly:

**When to use it:**
- Current progression feels stale
- Want to match a different mood
- Exploring musical variations quickly
- The current key doesn't vibe with your ambient selections

<Tip>
  Spam the refresh button 3-5 times rapidly, then listen to each new progression for 8 bars. You'll quickly find gems.
</Tip>

### The "Layering Ladder"

Build complexity gradually over long sessions:

**Hour 1:** Main track only (65%)
**Hour 2:** Add rain (45%)
**Hour 3:** Add night ambience (35%)
**Hour 4:** Add thunder (25%), reduce rain to 35%
**Hour 5:** Switch to campfire instead of rain, keep thunder

This creates a naturally evolving soundscape that stays interesting without being distracting.

### Using LoFi Engine for Sleep

1. Set main track to 40-50% (subtle)
2. Enable WORLD mode for ever-changing variety
3. Add rain at 60% or jungle at 55%
4. Choose underwater white noise ambient track (9)
5. Select a darker background (easier on eyes)
6. Let it run - each transition will be different but soothing

<Note>
  Your browser/computer must remain active. Consider using a dedicated device or adjusting power settings to prevent sleep.
</Note>
