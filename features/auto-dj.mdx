The Auto DJ system intelligently manages your audio environment by automatically controlling ambient effects and background tracks based on your chosen immersion mode.

## Immersion Modes

Choose from four distinct modes that define your listening experience:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Music" icon="music">
    Pure procedural music with no ambient effects. Perfect for focused work.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Atmosphere" icon="cloud">
    Adds core weather and nature effects for enhanced immersion.
  </Card>
  <Card title="World" icon="globe">
    Full immersion with all effects and ambient tracks enabled.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manual" icon="hand">
    Complete user control - Auto DJ makes no automatic changes.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Mode Details

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Music Mode">
    **Just the beat and chords. No atmosphere or world effects.**
    
    Ideal for:
    - Deep focus work
    - When you want minimal distraction
    - Pure musical background

    What's active:
    - ✅ Procedural music engine
    - ✅ Piano, drums, chords

    - ❌ Ambient tracks
    
    <Note>
    Music mode provides the cleanest audio experience with just the core LoFi beat.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Atmosphere Mode">
    **Adds the core weather and nature effects**
    
    Ideal for:
    - Studying or reading
    - Creative work
    - Relaxation
    - Background ambience
    
    What's active:
    - ✅ Procedural music engine
    - ✅ Rain and thunder effects
    - ✅ Core nature sounds
    - ❌ Ambient tracks (optional)
    
    <Tip>
    Atmosphere mode strikes the perfect balance between music and environmental immersion.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="World Mode">
    **Adds the specific textures like city, wind, etc.**
    
    Ideal for:
    - Maximum immersion
    - Long work sessions
    - Environmental escapism
    - Creative inspiration
    
    What's active:
    - ✅ Everything from Atmosphere mode
    - ✅ All ambient effects
    - ✅ Ambient tracks (city, train, waves, etc.)
    - ✅ Additional environmental layers

  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Manual Mode">
    **No automatic changes. Full user control.**
    
    Ideal for:
    - Custom soundscape creation
    - Precise control over every element
    - Experimenting with combinations
    - Advanced users
    
    Behavior:
    - ⚙️ Auto DJ makes no automatic changes
    - ⚙️ All effects remain in their current state
    - ⚙️ User controls everything via keyboard/UI
    - ⚙️ Perfect for creating custom mixes
    
    <Note>
    Manual mode gives you complete control but requires more active management.
    </Note>
  </Tab>

## Switching Modes

Change immersion modes in the Settings panel:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Press `J` to open the settings panel
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find Immersion Section">
    Look for the "Immersion" section with four mode buttons
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Mode">
    Click any mode button to activate it:
    - Music
    - Atmosphere
    - World
    - Manual
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Auto-Apply">
    The mode activates immediately and adjusts all effects accordingly
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Persistent Settings

Your mode selection is automatically saved:

<Accordion title="How It Works">
  - Selected mode is stored in `localStorage`
  - Persists across app restarts
  - No account or cloud sync required
  - Instant restore on launch
</Accordion>

## Technical Implementation

The Auto DJ system uses custom events to coordinate changes:

```typescript
window.dispatchEvent(
  new CustomEvent("auto-dj-mode-changed", { 
    detail: { mode: "ATMOSPHERE" } 
  })
);
```

Components listen for mode changes and adjust their behavior accordingly.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

While Auto DJ manages effects automatically, you can always override:

- Press `A`, `S`, `D`, `F` to toggle individual effects
- Press `1-9` to control ambient tracks
- Press `K` to stop all ambient sounds

Manual controls work in all modes except Manual mode (where they're the only control method).
