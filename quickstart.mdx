# Quick start

This guide will get you from installation to generating your first LoFi track in just a few minutes.

<Info>
If you haven't installed LoFi Engine yet, see the [installation guide](/installation) first.
</Info>

## Your first track

<Steps>
  <Step title="Launch LoFi Engine">
    Open the application:
    - **Linux**: Find it in your application menu or run `lofi-engine`
    - **macOS**: Open from Applications folder
    - **Windows**: Launch from Start Menu or Desktop shortcut
    - **Web**: Visit [lofi-engine.vercel.app](https://lofi-engine.vercel.app/)

    You'll see the main interface with a beautiful background artwork and the play button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start playback">
    Click the large play button in the center, or press the **Space** key.

    <Tip>
    The play button is your main control. Press Space at any time to play or pause.
    </Tip>

    LoFi Engine will immediately start generating a unique LoFi track using procedural generation powered by Tone.js. Every time you play, the experience is different.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the interface">
    The main interface consists of:

    - **Top bar**: Language selector and settings
    - **Track list** (left side): Ambient sound controls
    - **Controls** (right side): Immersion modes and effects
    - **Play button** (center): Main playback control

    <Note>
    Press **ESC** at any time to open the info box with all available keyboard shortcuts and app information.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose an Immersion mode">
    The Immersion system (Auto DJ) manages your soundscape automatically. Try different modes:

    - **Music**: Pure LoFi beats and chords without effects
    - **Atmosphere**: Adds weather and nature sounds
    - **World**: Includes city textures and environmental sounds
    - **Manual**: Full control to customize everything yourself

    Click the mode buttons in the Controls panel to switch between them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding ambient sounds

Enhance your track with ambient sounds:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse ambient tracks">
    Look at the Track List panel on the left side of the screen. You'll find various ambient sound options:

    - Nature sounds (birds, rain, waves)
    - Weather effects (wind, thunder)
    - Environmental sounds (city noise, cafe ambience)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Toggle tracks">
    Click on any ambient track to enable or disable it. Active tracks will be highlighted.

    <Tip>
    Use keyboard shortcuts to toggle tracks without clicking. Press ESC to view all shortcuts.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Adjust the mix">
    Switch to **Manual** mode in the Immersion settings for full control over which ambient tracks are active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Immersion modes

The Immersion system offers four distinct experiences:

### Music mode
Focuses purely on the procedurally generated LoFi beat and chords. No atmospheric effects, just clean music. Perfect for focused work.

### Atmosphere mode
Adds core weather and nature effects to the music for a fuller, more immersive sound. Great for studying or relaxing.

### World mode
Incorporates specific environmental textures like city sounds, wind, and more detailed ambient layers. Creates deep immersion for creative work.

### Manual mode
Gives you complete control. Mix and match any combination of music, effects, and ambient tracks. Be the artist and create your unique soundscape.

<Info>
You can switch between modes at any time while playing. Experiment to find what works best for your current activity.
</Info>

## Working offline

One of LoFi Engine's key features is complete offline functionality:

- No internet connection required after installation
- Your listening experience is fully private
- All audio generation happens locally using Tone.js
- No data is collected or transmitted

<Note>
The web version requires an internet connection, but the desktop app works entirely offline.
</Note>
